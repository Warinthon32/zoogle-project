African Lion

Panthera leo

The African lion is one of the largest big cats and is known for its strength, social behavior, and iconic roar. They live in groups called prides and are top predators in their ecosystem.

Description

The African lion is a large carnivorous mammal native to sub-Saharan Africa. Adult males are easily recognized by their thick manes, which vary in color and size depending on age and genetics. Lions are unique among big cats because they live in social groups known as prides.

Diet & Nutrition

  • Diet Type Carnivore - feeds on large mammals such as zebras, wildebeest, buffalo, and antelope
  • Food Info Fed with fresh meat such as beef or chicken, provided in controlled portions to match natural feeding behavior
  • Feeding Quantity Approximately 5-7 kg per day (varies by age and size)

Where to Find

  • Zone Savanna Zone
  • Exhibit Open enclosure with rocks, shade areas, and water sources to mimic natural environment
